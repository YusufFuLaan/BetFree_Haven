How gambling affects your life

Gambling can affect many areas of your life
A financial crisis is often what brings a person to address their gambling. It’s also not uncommon for partners, friends or family members to tell us that they did not realize their loved one was experiencing gambling harms until there were serious financial consequences such as a court summons for non-payment of debts, or a repossession action on their home. Financial problems can really mount up, and more quickly than you realize. Bills don’t get paid, credit cards are maxed out, debts accumulate – payday loans look like a solution, but high interest rates make the situation worse. Borrowing or stealing from loved ones, businesses or employers also happens when people feel increasingly desperate.

Shared stories

I found a support group that changed my life. With the guidance of counsellors and the support of fellow members, I learned to set boundaries, like limiting my time and money spent on gambling. It was hard at first, but gradually, I started to reclaim my life ...
