Block Betting Websites

Blocking betting websites is an effective way to maintain control over your gaming habits and prevent problem gambling. With the rise of online betting, it's easier than ever to access betting sites, but this convenience can also lead to addiction. Taking control of your digital habits starts with blocking access to gambling sites—protect your time, focus, and financial well-being.

Methods to block betting websites

Here are some methods to block betting websites; DNS Blocking: Internet Service Providers (ISPs) can block access to specific domains by preventing users from resolving the website’s domain name, making it impossible to access the site.

IP Blocking: Websites can be blocked by targeting their IP addresses, preventing users from accessing servers associated with the gambling websites.

Blacklists: ISPs or software applications maintain blacklists of known gambling websites and block access to these sites for users attempting to visit them.

VPN Detection: Some gambling websites use software to detect the use of VPNs, which are often used by users to bypass restrictions. These detection measures can block users who attempt to use VPNs to access the sites.

Browser Extensions and Apps: Users can install apps or browser extensions that block access to specific websites. These can be used by individuals who are trying to limit their exposure to gambling platforms.
